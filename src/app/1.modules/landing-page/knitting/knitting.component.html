Our Process

Cassandra Harada has been knitting and making garments with natural fibers for over 22 years. She has been designing for publications like “Keitodama” and contributing to seasonal collections for yarn companies like Jamieson’s of Shetland for 15 years. While designing patterns for original knitwear is a joy, Cassandra really prefers to work with singular customers who are in need of something specific to suit their needs.

Reach out to Cassandra via our form or at cassandra@cinema-tokyo.com to discuss your needs and schedule a fitting.

Garments can also be made from measurements or sending a well loved item that fits, in order to achieve a specific shape and silhouette.
