 Allowances 

We make every effort to ensure that our clients are 100% satisfied with the garment they receive. Due to the nature of the product and production it is possible that some flaws may occur. If there are any adjustments required, we will do our best to make them so customers are pleased with the end product but some tiny imperfections are inevitable in handmade goods.   

We make every effort to ensure that the cloth we produce is of the highest quality and all cloth is inspected before dispatch. Due to the nature of the product it is possible that some flaws may occur which cannot be mended. Cloth will be supplied with any flaws tagged and an industry standard allowance provided. Allowances are given as 0.2m per horizontal flaw and flaw length for vertical flaws. 

Claims: All garments are carefully inspected prior to despatch. The buyer should also inspect the finished garment carefully on arrival. All claims must be reported within 10 business days. Failure to make such claims within the specified time constitutes acceptance of the garments and quality thereof.   

However, Every garment produced in house is subject to free lifetime mending (excluding shipping costs) so if something should be damaged with regular use, please contact us and return the garment for repair. We will retain or provide yarn for mending free of charge within reason.
